Welcome to GitHub Pages.

ReportIt was established by three Swarthmore Students with a desire to strengthen the voices of people within communities. Have a complaint about something that you believe the government should know about? Want to know what problems are happening within your community and try to pool together resources to solve it? ReportIt.

Built by Razi Shaban (@rrshaban), Sedinam Worlanyo (@sedinam), and Amy Han (@raizel17).

ReportIt is built with Flask, SQLite3, Jinja2, gmaps.js, jQuery, and Bootstrap.

Reportit maintained by rrshaban
